The Law of Conservation of Energy

Thelaw of conservation of energystates that energy is neither created nor destroyed in an isolated system ‚Äì it can only change form.

Anisolated systemis a system that is completely isolated from outside energy and matter. It does not allow anything to pass into it.

Aclosed systemis a system that only allows energy to pass into it.

Anopen systemis a system that allows both energy and matter to pass into it.

In an isolated system, energy is neither created nor destroyed, and it can only change form. This is the law of conservation of energy.
