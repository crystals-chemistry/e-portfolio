Kinematics

Kinematics is the subfield of physics that deals with the motion of a body or a system of bodies. However, kinematics does not consider the forces that produce the motion. In our curriculum, we learned about motion in one dimension, motion in two dimensions, projectile motion, and relative motion.

Circular Motion

Uniform circular motion is the motion of an object with a constant speed along a circular path that has a constant radius. Objects that move with uniform circular motion are in non-inertial frames of reference.
